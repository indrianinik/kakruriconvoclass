📌
A note for every session
Each session ends with one question for you: "What is one thing you want to try this week?" — spoken out loud, not written. This builds the metacognitive habit that separates B2 students who plateau from those who reach C1. Session 10 is a deliberate pivot point: use it freely to adjust, drop, or extend any topic based on what's actually emerged. Use real Australian university contexts wherever possible — ANU, Melbourne, Monash, UQ. The more specific the roleplay, the more useful it is.
Made for one student, preparing for something big. Good luck. 🇦🇺
